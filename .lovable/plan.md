
# FinLog – Business Accounting Landing Page

A sleek, fintech-inspired landing page for FinLog, a business accounting app based in Bangladesh.

## Design
- **Dark theme** with neon/accent highlights (teal/cyan gradients)
- Modern typography, glassmorphism cards, smooth scroll animations
- Bangladesh-focused imagery and BDT currency references

## Sections

1. **Navbar** – Logo, nav links (Features, How It Works, Pricing, FAQ), "Get Started" CTA button

2. **Hero** – Bold headline ("Smart Accounting for Bangladeshi Businesses"), subtext, CTA buttons ("Start Free Trial" + "Watch Demo"), hero illustration/mockup of the app dashboard

3. **Trusted By / Stats** – Logos or stats bar (e.g., "5,000+ businesses", "৳500Cr+ tracked", "99.9% uptime")

4. **Features** – Icon cards highlighting: Invoicing & Billing, Expense Tracking, Financial Reports, Tax Compliance (VAT/AIT), Multi-branch Support, Bank Reconciliation

5. **How It Works** – 3-step visual flow: Sign Up → Connect Your Accounts → Get Insights

6. **Testimonials** – Cards with quotes from sample Bangladeshi business owners

7. **Pricing** – 3-tier cards (Starter, Professional, Enterprise) with BDT pricing

8. **FAQ** – Accordion with common questions about the app

9. **CTA Section** – Final call-to-action banner ("Ready to simplify your finances?")

10. **Footer** – Links, contact info, social media, "Made in Bangladesh 🇧🇩"
